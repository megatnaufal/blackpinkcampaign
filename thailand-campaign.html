Music Schools for Art Education

What BLACKPINK Did for This Campaign

Thailand, Songkhla

Blackpink’s Music School for Art Education campaign, launched in Songkhla, Thailand, is an exciting and unique initiative designed to offer young people a comprehensive introduction to the world of music. The campaign targets individuals aged 10 to 20, providing them with a structured curriculum that covers everything from the basics of music to advanced techniques. With Blackpink as the driving force behind this project, the program aims to inspire and empower the next generation of musicians in Thailand, encouraging creativity and artistic expression.

This initiative provides participants with valuable knowledge and hands-on experience in various areas of music, including theory, performance, and production. They will have the opportunity to learn from seasoned industry professionals, gaining insights into both the technical and creative aspects of music. Through this program, young individuals can explore their passion for music in a nurturing and educational environment.

In addition to developing their musical talents, this campaign fosters a deeper appreciation for the arts. It encourages the youth of Thailand to pursue their musical dreams, empowering them to contribute to the vibrant cultural landscape of the future.
