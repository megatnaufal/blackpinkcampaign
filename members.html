BLACKPINK MEMBERS

Jisoo

Full Name: Kim Ji-soo

Born: January 3, 1995 (Age: 29)

Role: Lead Vocalist, Visual

Jisoo, the eldest member of BLACKPINK, is known for her elegance, humor, and captivating visuals. Often referred to as the "visual queen" of the group, Jisoo has also ventured into acting, starring in the Korean drama "Snowdrop."

Fun Fact: Jisoo loves painting and is known for her witty personality!

Jennie

Full Name: Jennie Kim

Born: January 16, 1996 (Age: 28)

Role: Main Rapper, Lead Vocalist

Raised in New Zealand, Jennie is known for her chic and confident stage presence. As the first member to release a solo track titled "SOLO," she continues to inspire fans with her versatility in music and fashion.

Fun Fact: Jennie has a passion for photography and owns multiple vintage cameras.

Rosé

Full Name: Park Chae-young (Roséanne Park)

Born: February 11, 1997 (Age: 27)

Role: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer

Rosé was born in New Zealand and raised in Australia. Her unique vocal tone and emotional delivery have made her a standout performer. She’s also skilled at playing guitar and piano, adding a personal touch to her music.

Fun Fact: Rosé's favorite food is avocado toast!

Lisa

Full Name: Lalisa Manoban

Born: March 27, 1997 (Age: 27)

Role: Main Dancer, Lead Rapper

Hailing from Thailand, Lisa is the group's main dancer and lead rapper. Her outstanding dance skills have earned her recognition as one of the best dancers in K-pop. In addition, her solo career has seen incredible success with hits like "LALISA."

Fun Fact: Lisa is fluent in Thai, Korean, English, and Japanese!
