Discover YG Entertainment

About YG Entertainment

Founded in 1996 by Yang Hyun-suk, YG Entertainment has grown to become a leading force in the K-pop industry. Known for its innovation, YG Entertainment has pioneered a genre-defying approach to music, blending hip-hop, pop, and R&B influences to create global sensations like BIGBANG, 2NE1, BLACKPINK, and more.

As one of the "Big Three" entertainment companies, YG Entertainment continues to dominate the entertainment landscape, with its artists consistently breaking records and redefining the global pop culture scene.

Our Location

Located in the heart of Seoul, South Korea, YG Entertainment's headquarters is a hub of creativity, where musical innovation and artistry thrive. As one of the most influential agencies in K-pop, YG is strategically placed at the center of global trends in entertainment and fashion.

Our Journey

YG Entertainment's journey began with the success of artists like Sechs Kies and Jinusean, which laid the foundation for a music empire. In the years since its establishment, YG has expanded its roster to include some of the most iconic groups in K-pop history, including BIGBANG, 2NE1, and BLACKPINK. The company's history is marked by its unwavering commitment to pushing boundaries and evolving with the times.

Meet Our Founder

Yang Hyun-suk, the visionary founder and CEO of YG Entertainment, has played an instrumental role in shaping the company's direction. Formerly a member of the influential group Seo Taiji and Boys, Yang's deep understanding of the industry, combined with his creative approach to music production, has led YG Entertainment to global prominence.

What We Do

At YG Entertainment, we specialize in discovering, developing, and nurturing world-class talent. Our artists are known for their unparalleled performances, genre-defying music, and global influence. In addition to artist management, YG is involved in music production, international collaborations, fashion, and event management, making us a central player in both the K-pop and global entertainment industries.

  • Vision: To be the world's leading entertainment company, inspiring generations with groundbreaking music, performances, and creativity.
  • Mission: To nurture and promote top-tier talent, innovate within the global music industry, and create iconic cultural moments through music and entertainment.
  • Core Values:

    • Excellence: Striving for perfection in every aspect of entertainment.
    • Global Influence: Bringing K-pop and Korean culture to the world.
  • Achievements:
