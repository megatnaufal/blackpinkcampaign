BLACKPINK

BLACKPINK is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment

BLACKPINK debuted in 2016 and has become one of the most iconic K-pop groups globally. Known for their powerful performances, catchy music, and fierce fashion, the group has set multiple records and reached global stardom. The members are Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa.

  • Album: Blackpink in Your Area (2018)

  • Single: Kill This Love (2019)
